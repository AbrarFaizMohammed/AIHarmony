### About The Project
ðŸš€ Excited to share my latest project on Aiharmony! ðŸ¤– **AI's are very helpful and time-saving** when it comes to getting information by searching online. They guide your search, resulting in decreased search time or providing the expected results you're looking for. However, comparing results generated by different AI models can be challenging. To address this, I've developed **Aiharmony**, a powerful chat application that tackles two critical issues:

1. **Comparing AI Responses**: Aiharmony allows you to compare any two AI responses simultaneously. This feature helps you evaluate the quality and relevance of answers from different models.

2. **Enhancing Data Privacy**: As middleware, Aiharmony filters out sensitive information from prompts before sending them to OpenAI and Gemini platforms. This ensures data privacy and prevents confidential details from being exposed.
